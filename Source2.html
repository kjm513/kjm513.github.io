Source 2 is the successor of the Source engine. It has been in development since 2007, with the first private tech demo being a map from left 4 dead 2 being remade in the engine. The first public release for Source 2 was when Valve ported dota 2 to the new engine, this was mainly for developing further developing the engine and testing out features before they ship out a major release.
